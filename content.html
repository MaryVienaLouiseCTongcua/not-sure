    • 2. Cutting down Forests: Due to increase of cutting of trees, this causes to take away the tree's ability to keep out emissions out of our atmosphere. This would lead to more carbon dioxide being released and absorbed.

    • 2. Severe storms: Since the temperature has increased, then that would mean the more moisture evaporates. Its been said that more storms had occurred more often, this would lead to destruction of homes and deaths.
    • 3 Health Risks: The impacts of Climate Change is starting to create pollution and diseases which can affect an individual's health both on the mental and physical health.

As ocean temperatures rises as well, corals are starting to lose their color and started to slowly die. Not only doe this affect their habitats, but also the food and breeding cycle of the species.
